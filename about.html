Who We Are

GBfoods Ghana is a leading Fast-Moving Consumer Goods (FMCG) company dedicated to providing high-quality, locally produced culinary solutions for every Ghanaian household. As part of the global GBfoods group, we combine international expertise with a deep understanding of local taste preferences.

Company Profile

Year Established 2013
Headquarters Airport City, Accra, Ghana
Major Plant Opposite POLYKRAFT LTD, Heavy Industrial Area, Tema
Parent Company GBfoods International
Key Brands Gino, Jumbo, Pomo, Jago
Industry Food & Beverage Manufacturing

Our Core Values

Mission

To provide high-quality, locally produced culinary solutions for every Ghanaian home, while supporting local agriculture and communities.

Vision

To be Ghana's most trusted food brand, promoting local agriculture, sustainable food production, and nourishing families across the nation.

Target Audience

Households, caterers, restaurants, supermarkets, and food vendors across Ghana and beyond.

Our History

2013

GBfoods Ghana was established in Accra, bringing the trusted Gino brand to Ghanaian consumers and setting up a state-of-the-art processing plant in Tema.

2015

Expanded product lines to include Jumbo seasoning cubes and Pomo tomato mix, rapidly growing market presence across the country.

2018

Launched the Jago mayonnaise brand and introduced Gino pasta products, cementing GBfoods as a full-range culinary solutions provider.

2022

Invested in major plant upgrades, increasing production capacity and creating hundreds of local jobs in the Tema Industrial Area.

2024

Launched the Gino Jollof Mix and expanded the Herbs & Spices range, responding to growing consumer demand for convenient cooking solutions.
